IT-RELATED BACHELOR PROGRAMS OVERVIEW

1. BSc. CSIT (Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology)
- Affiliating University: Tribhuvan University (TU)
- Duration: 4 years (8 semesters)
- Focus: Strong theoretical foundation in computer science - data structures, algorithms, operating systems, computer networks, AI, machine learning, and a mandatory entrance exam.
- Entrance: TU CSIT entrance exam (Physics, Chemistry, Math, English, IQ).

2. BIT (Bachelor of Information Technology)
- Affiliating University: Purbanchal University (PU) or similar
- Duration: 4 years
- Focus: More application/industry oriented, covers programming, web/mobile development, database systems, and IT project management.

3. BCA (Bachelor of Computer Application)
- Affiliating University: TU, PU
- Duration: 4 years
- Focus: Application development, software engineering, business computing.

4. BE Computer Engineering
- Affiliating University: TU (IOE), Pokhara University
- Duration: 4 years
- Focus: Hardware + software, embedded systems, along with core programming and networking.

5. Approximate Fee Range (illustrative, varies by college and changes yearly - always confirm directly with the college)
- Community/government-affiliated colleges: lower fees, often NPR 3-6 lakhs total for the whole program.
- Private colleges: NPR 6-12+ lakhs total for the whole program, depending on facilities and location.
- Additional costs: lab fees, project fees, industrial tour, exam fees per semester.

6. Choosing Between Programs
- If interested in research, AI/ML, or higher studies abroad: BSc.CSIT or BE Computer are generally preferred due to stronger theoretical base.
- If interested in quick industry entry as a developer: BIT/BCA offer more hands-on, practical exposure.
- Always check the specific college's syllabus and industry tie-ups since quality varies significantly between colleges even under the same affiliating university.
